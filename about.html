About Shawarma Snack Center

A small business, one skewer at a time.

Our Story

Shawarma Snack Center started as a single street cart before growing into a neighborhood favorite. Every recipe on our menu, from the garlic sauce to the spice rub on our skewers, is still made the same way it was on day one.

We keep things simple: fresh ingredients, fast service, and a menu that doesn't change just because it's already good.
